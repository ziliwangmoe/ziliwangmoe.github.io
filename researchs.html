My own idea about how can the artificial neural system work out

Let me take the image recognition as an example. There are many important issues in the mapping between the image and a word. Like transform invariance or inference. In my understanding, these issues cannot be addressed only by classification. Classification is just a regular module in the whole process of image-word mapping. In this article I will describe each module in artificial neural system. These are my own immature ideas. I wrote down just for discussion and recording.
